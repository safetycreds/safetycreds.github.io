SafetyCreds

SafetyCreds tracks the safety behaviour of subscribing users — starting with safe driving, then healthy activity — and turns it into safety credits that can be redeemed as insurance benefits with partnering insurance companies. Your safe behaviour, finally measured, owned by you, and rewarded.

01

Subscribe & consent

Register and choose exactly which safety signals you share. Everything is opt-in and can be paused anytime.

02

Behave safely

Drive smoothly and distraction-free, stay active, complete verified safety actions — tracked automatically.

03

Earn credits

Verified safe behaviour becomes safety credits and a personal safety score. Rewards only — never penalties.

04

Claim benefits

Redeem credits with partnering insurers for premium discounts, cashback, and wellness perks.
